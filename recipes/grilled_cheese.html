Grilled Cheese

Home

Description

Elevate your pasta dishes with fresh homemade pesto

Ingredients

  • 2x slices bread
  • 0.25x cup of gouda cheese
  • 0.25x cup of cheder cheese
  • 0.25x cup of mozzarella cheese
  • Butter as needed

Steps

  1. Mix cheese in bowl.
  2. Add nob of butter to pan and toast one side of each piece of bread.
  3. Add cheese to toasted side of bread and cover with other slice.
  4. Add more butter and toast remaining side of grilled cheese.
  5. enjoy.
