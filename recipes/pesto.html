Pesto

Home

Description

Elevate your pasta dishes with fresh homemade pesto

Ingredients

  • 1x bush/cup of fresh basil leaves
  • 3x cloves of garlic
  • 1x cup of grated parmesan
  • 0.25x cup of pine nuts
  • 0.25x cup of olive oil
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Put blender in fridge to chill for 20min.
  2. toast pine nuts on low heat on stove.
  3. Put all Ingredients in blender and mix until smooth.
  4. Season to taste.
