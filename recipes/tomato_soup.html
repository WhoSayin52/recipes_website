Tomato Soup

Home

Description

Succulent tomato soup for a nice winter evening.

Best paired with norther than a butter grilled cheese.

Ingredients

  • 2x 250g tomato cans
  • 2x medium bell peppers
  • 2x medium yellow onions
  • 1x full head of garlic
  • 100ml of heavy cream
  • herbs
  • olive oil as needed
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Add veggies and herbs to tray. Drizzle with oil and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Put tray in 210C oven until the vegetables start to char.
  3. Put vegetables in a pot, and add tomato cans.
  4. Cook until soft and then mix with hand blender until smooth.
  5. (optional) sieve soup through strainer for extra creamy texture.
  6. Turn off stove and mix in cream.
  7. (optional) Add cream for looks and serve with grilled cheese.
